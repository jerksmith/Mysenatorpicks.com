Luke Espirito

Luke Espirito is a labor lawyer, activist, and advocate for workers' rights in the Philippines. He has been a strong voice against contractualization, low wages, and unjust labor practices, consistently pushing for policies that protect the working class. As a leader in labor movements and a staunch defender of human rights, Espirito has dedicated his career to fighting for economic justice, fair employment conditions, and social equality. His platform focuses on ensuring workers receive dignified wages, job security, and better social services, making him a candidate who prioritizes the needs of ordinary Filipinos.
